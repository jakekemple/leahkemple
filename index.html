About Me

I’m a journalist who is looking for the best way to gain writing experience and overall knowledge about the world. I specialize in travel, political, and general news writing as well as marketing and public relations writing. I have been traveling the world with my family since I was three years old, and I plan on keeping this up for the rest of my life. Though I’ve seen the beaches of Mexico, the mountains of Santorini and the wildlife of Africa, I crave more knowledge, and I plan on spending the rest of my life learning about the world, its beauty, and its people.
